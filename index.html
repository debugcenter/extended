About

The Khan Acedemy Discord server was created so that
learners on Khan Academy can chat with each other
without the rescrictions of Khan Academy getting in the way.
However, you still have to follow rules. Our server offers
self assignable roles, a verification system, subscription
channels, and more. We even have a custom bot. We have an
active and friendly staff team and server logs are open for
members to view. Enjoy your stay!

What's Discord?

Discord is a proprietary freeware VoIP application and digital
distribution platform designed for video gaming
communities, that specializes in text, image, video and
audio communication between users in a chat channel.
You can download the app on your computer or phone
or just use the browser version.You can live chat with
anybody on it and create serversfor you and your friends.
